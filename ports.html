About (SIMI)

Samson Ishaya Ministries International (SIMI) is a prophetic global movement committed to identifying, training, and releasing Kingdom leaders into excellence across the spheres of ministry, marketplace, governance, and enterprise. Founded on a divine calling to marry spiritual depth with strategic impact, SIMI operates from a place of apostolic authority and global vision.

Our Vision

We pursue a world transformed by empowered leaders—equipped with revelation, faith, and excellence—to transform nations, restore systems, and release Divine purpose in every sphere of society.

Our Mission

Raise spiritually rooted, marketplace-savvy leaders
Build a network of apostolic hubs across continents
Launch transformational initiatives in governance, business, and the church
Cultivate prophetic culture, practical excellence, and global collaboration

Core Values

Kingdom Strategy: Spiritual power meets practical strategy
Prophetic Foundation: Hearing God's voice and leading with grace
Global Reach: An international mandate to impact every nation
Excellence & Integrity: Kingdom standards in every effort

What We Do

Institute

At Samson Ishaya Ministries International, we believe in raising leaders who are not only spiritually grounded but also economically empowered. Our dual-track system — the School of Ministry and the School of Enterprise — is designed to equip believers for dominion in both the pulpit and the marketplace.

School Of Ministry

The School of Ministry is a dynamic training ground for emerging spiritual leaders. It focuses on:
Sound doctrine, prophetic insight, and apostolic boldness
Ministerial ethics, church planting, and revival leadership
Building strong altars, effective prayer lives, and supernatural living
Goal: To raise fire-filled, Word-grounded leaders who are equipped to disciple nations.

School of Enterprise

The School of Enterprise empowers Kingdom-minded individuals to thrive in business and leadership. It delivers:
Kingdom principles for wealth creation and stewardship
Training in innovation, digital enterprise, and systems thinking
Practical skills in entrepreneurship, leadership, and financial intelligence
Goal: To raise financially literate, enterprise-driven believers who fund and influence Kingdom agendas globally.
